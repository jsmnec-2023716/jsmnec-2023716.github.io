About Cats


Even though I've never had a cat, I would consider myself a cat person. But did you know that
people are allergic to cats because of their saliva and not their fur? It's because cats cover themselves in their saliva
in order to clean themselves and that is what makes people allergic to it. The more you know!!

Fun fact: I didn't know that about cats until today. My friend's little brother told me while I was at her house.
I just thought that that was interesting to think about so I wanted to share that here :)
